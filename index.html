AIEFAS

Artificial Intelligence Enabled Fugitive Apprehension System (AIEFAS) is a project that I developed and presented for my final year project seminar. The project makes use of Convolutional Neural Networks to recongize faces of interest (fugutives). Once a face has been recognized an alert is sent to a system admin in this case a police officer. The goal of the project is to use AI to reduce the number of criminals that are not apprehended.

UJ Community Engagement

Year in, year out the University of Johannesburg is involved in community engagement projects. For their latest iteration in 2021, I was asked to develop an educational application for android that would teach an entire course on sustainable development. The app had to have multiple features such as an online database so that users can sign in and out. Users were also supposed to be able to comment on certain course content. The app had an integrated quiz feature so that students can test their knowledge and us as the administrators could see how well they understand the course content..
